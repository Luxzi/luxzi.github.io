Who am I?


My name is Luxzi or Lux for short. I've been writing software for about 8 years (since 2016). My projects consist of low level systems software, game modding, utilites, and many other things. You will find some of my best work below.

What is the future of this website?


I plan to add a blog with an RSS feed and other sections to this site. When: TBD.

What got me into computer science?


Ever since I was little I've always been interested in the inner workings of electronics. I would disassemble and reassemble electronics to find out how they worked. I would look around the Windows filesystem trying to figure out what each little thing did. Then, I discovered Linux. It was an operating system that I could customize to my hearts content. This discovery eventually led to me learning C, C++, and later Rust when it hit 1.0. I also took up web development and learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
